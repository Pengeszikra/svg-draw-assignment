## Great Feature from vector drawing application:

  - *complex vector fill* (Flash, AdobeIllustrator)

  - *frame by frame animation* (Flash)

  - *auto merging* (Flash)

  - *layer* (PhotoShop)

  - *art board* (PhotoShop)

  - *blob* (boxy-svg)[https://boxy-svg.com/app]

  - *auto snap* (Adobe Illustrator)
